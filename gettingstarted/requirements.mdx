---
title: "System Requirements"
---
HeatmapHero's cloud-based nature ensures compatibility across various systems and devices. 

The system requirements for integrating HeatmapHero are minimal

Compatible with all major web browsers
(Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)

Supports any web-based platform, irrespective of the technology used (HTML, JavaScript, etc.)

Requires an active internet connection for data transfer to the HeatmapHero servers.
