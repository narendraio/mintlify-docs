Heatmaphero provides dedicated customer support to assist users with any queries or issues they might encounter while using the platform.

## Support Contact Information

**Email Support** 

Users can reach out to Heatmaphero's customer support team via email at support@Heatmaphero.com for any assistance or inquiries.

**Live Chat** 

Users have access to a live chat support feature available on the Heatmaphero website or within the dashboard for real-time assistance.
